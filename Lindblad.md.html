Lindblad Equation

The Note of Lindblad Equation

The Fermi Golden rule

The System and bath model

Base on A simple derivation of Lindblad equation Consider a System interact with a bath, the Hamiltonian of system is \hat{H}_S and the bath is \hat{H}_B. And the total Hamiltonian is \hat{H}=\hat{H}_S \otimes \hat{1}_{B} + \hat{1}_{S} \otimes \hat{H}_B + \alpha\hat{H}_{SB} \qquad(1)

Where \hat{H}_{SB} is \hat{H}_{SB} = \hbar(\hat{S}^{\dagger}\hat{B}+\hat{S}\hat{B}^{\dagger}). \qquad(2)
